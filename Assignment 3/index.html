CN Tower is 9th tallest free standing structure in the world and it is 1,815.3ft high .it was created in 1975 and was declared a one of the seven wonders of the world in 1995.now it has become a signature icon of the skyline of toronto and a very popular landmark in canada attracting nearly 2 million tourists anually. The CN tower was several observation decks , a revolving restaurant at nearly 350m above ground and an entertainment complex. for trill seekers there is an EdgeWalk at 356m high where tourists are tethered to a rail system and after which they can freely walk around the main pod. Royal Ontario museum

Located in north of queen's park , The royal ontario museum of art is the largest museum in North America. over hundred years old museum welcomes over a million visitors every year making it the most visited museum in canada. the museum displays nearly 6,000,000 items and 40 galleries giving it its reputation at an international level.It houses the world's largest collection of fossil consisting of more than 150,000 specimens. the museum also displays extraorginary collection of textile art, metal art , rock art and oil paintings. if you are an admirer of art and culture then the royal ontario museum is surely worth the visit.

It also has areas such as the Kids Zoo, Waterside Theatre, and Splash Island. it has one of the most diverse collection of species; currently displays over 5,000 animals .
